ABERRATION DOCUMENT: ABR-014 "The Echoing Whisperer"

Aberration Designation: ABR-014 "The Echoing Whisperer"

Threat Level: Class IV: Critical

Containment Level: Delta-1

Description

ABR-014, colloquially referred to as "The Echoing Whisperer," is a vintage, ribbon-style microphone with a tarnished silver exterior and a delicate mesh grill. The microphone emits an ethereal glow when active, and the sound it captures is imbued with anomalous properties. When spoken or sung into, ABR-014 amplifies the sound, adding a haunting reverberation that carries an unsettling resonance capable of affecting both physical and mental states.

The echoes produced by ABR-014 are not mere distortions but seem to carry an imprint of intent. These echoes have been documented to induce profound psychological effects, including heightened paranoia, auditory hallucinations, and in extreme cases, a compulsion to mimic or obey the whispered commands embedded within the reverberations. Subjects exposed to the echoes often report hearing voices urging them to perform dangerous or nonsensical actions.

ABR-014's influence extends beyond its immediate vicinity. Recordings made with the microphone retain its anomalous effects, making the device particularly dangerous if mishandled. Prolonged exposure to these recordings has led to cases of irreversible mental degradation and severe cognitive dissonance.

Containment Protocols

ABR-014 is to be housed within a soundproof, anechoic chamber at ARI Site-77. The chamber must be constructed with multiple layers of dense, non-resonant materials to prevent any sound leakage. Entry to the chamber requires Level 4 clearance, and all personnel must undergo psychological screening before and after exposure.

All interactions with ABR-014 must be conducted using remote-controlled equipment. Any audio recordings produced by ABR-014 are to be classified as Level 5 restricted and stored in a separate, soundproof vault. Under no circumstances should ABR-014 be connected to any external audio broadcasting systems.

In the event of a containment breach, the sector must be evacuated immediately, and sound-dampening protocols activated. Mobile task forces equipped with active noise cancellation technology will be deployed to re-secure the aberration.

Risk Assessment

ABR-014 poses a severe threat due to its ability to manipulate auditory perception and induce harmful psychological states. The unpredictable nature of its echoes and the potential for widespread mental influence make ABR-014 a critical containment priority. Its ability to embed anomalous properties into recordings necessitates strict control over all media produced using the device.

Behavioral Analysis

ABR-014 exhibits a reactive activation pattern, often responding to ambient sound levels or direct vocal input. The echoes generated appear to have an underlying pattern or intelligence, suggesting the presence of a sentient or semi-sentient element. Further analysis is required to understand the full scope of ABR-014's capabilities and the potential origins of its anomalous properties.

Testing has revealed that the psychological impact of ABR-014's echoes is cumulative, with repeated exposure leading to more severe symptoms. The aberration's influence appears to be particularly potent in individuals with pre-existing psychological conditions, though no one is entirely immune.

Appendices

Background: ABR-014 was discovered in an abandoned recording studio in ████████, USA, following a series of mysterious deaths attributed to unexplained auditory phenomena. The microphone was found connected to an antiquated recording system, still active despite the lack of power to the building. Local authorities were alerted to the abnormal circumstances, and ARI agents intervened to secure the aberration. Survivors from the initial discovery team reported persistent auditory hallucinations, necessitating the use of Class-B amnestics.

Incident Reports:

  • Incident 014-A: During initial recovery operations, an agent exposed to ABR-014's direct output experienced severe psychological breakdown, resulting in self-inflicted injuries. The agent was immediately evacuated and received intensive care, eventually recovering with lingering auditory sensitivity.
  • Incident 014-B: An unauthorized recording made with ABR-014 was inadvertently broadcasted within Site-77, leading to a mass panic and multiple cases of temporary psychosis. Emergency protocols were enacted, and the recording was subsequently destroyed.

Research Notes:

  • Experiment 014-01: Controlled tests with ABR-014 indicate that the anomalous echoes contain subsonic frequencies capable of influencing brainwave patterns. Subjects exposed to these frequencies demonstrate increased suggestibility and heightened emotional states.
  • Experiment 014-02: Attempts to analyze the structure of the echoes reveal complex waveforms with embedded data, suggesting a form of coded communication or influence. Deciphering these waveforms is a priority for ongoing research.

Proposal for Reclassification: Given the extensive psychological impact and potential for misuse, there are ongoing discussions to reclassify ABR-014 to Class V: Apocalyptic. This proposal is under review by the Executive Council, pending further research and analysis.
