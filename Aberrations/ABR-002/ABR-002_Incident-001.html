Incident Report: ABR-002 "Voidshadow"

Incident Title: Security Breach and Containment Failure - ABR-002 "Voidshadow"

Date of Incident: ██/██/████

Location: Containment Facility Delta-2, Aberration Research Institute (ARI)

ABR-002 in containment cell

Summary

On ██/██/████, a routine containment check for ABR-002, designated "Voidshadow," resulted in a critical breach. During the inspection, Researcher ██████, while calibrating the high-intensity LED lights within the containment chamber, inadvertently made direct contact with the entity. ABR-002 immediately initiated a series of neurological disruptions in the researcher, leading to a rapid escalation of symptoms and aggressive behavior.

Detailed Account

1. Pre-Incident Conditions

ABR-002 was secured within a 5m x 5m x 5m chamber, designed with high-density polymer and lined with high-intensity LED lights to ensure continuous illumination. The lighting system was functioning at the required 10,000 lumens to prevent ABR-002 from camouflaging. Regular maintenance protocols were in place, requiring periodic checks and recalibration of the lighting system by authorized personnel.

2. Incident Initiation

At 14:32, Researcher ██████ entered the containment chamber for routine recalibration of the lighting system. The researcher, equipped with standard reflective gear, began the calibration process near the central lighting control panel. At approximately 14:35, the researcher accidentally brushed against the containment cell wall, which had ABR-002 extended tendrils in proximity.

3. Immediate Effects (Phase 1: Disorientation)

Upon contact, Researcher ██████ reported feeling sudden disorientation, vertigo, and a profound sense of impending doom. Surveillance footage shows the researcher staggering, losing balance, and attempting to vocalize distress but failing due to escalating anxiety and confusion. These symptoms are consistent with Phase 1 of ABR-002’s neurological disruption effects.

4. Rapid Escalation (Phase 2: Neurological Overload)

Within 90 seconds, the researcher exhibited severe symptoms, including intense headaches, auditory hallucinations, and involuntary muscle spasms. The researcher was seen clutching his head and exhibiting erratic movements, indicating a rapid degradation of cognitive functions. Attempts to communicate with the researcher via the intercom system failed as he was unable to respond coherently.

5. Behavioral Alteration (Phase 3: Host Override)

By 14:38, the researcher’s behavior became highly aggressive and unpredictable. He began to assault the containment equipment, causing damage to the LED lighting array and other critical systems. Security personnel were alerted and dispatched to the containment chamber. They observed the researcher’s heightened strength and erratic behavior, indicative of ABR-002's influence. Despite attempts to subdue him using non-lethal methods, the researcher continued to resist with extraordinary force, damaging several containment protocols.

6. Containment Breach

The aggressive behavior led to significant damage to the LED lighting array, resulting in a partial failure of the illumination system within the containment chamber. ABR-002 utilized the reduced lighting to obscure itself, merging with the surrounding shadows and becoming nearly invisible to standard surveillance systems. The entity’s movement patterns became erratic, indicating an attempt to exploit the compromised containment environment.

7. Security Response and Recontainment

An immediate lockdown protocol was initiated, sealing off the containment wing to prevent ABR-002 from escaping the primary containment zone. Security teams equipped with full-body reflective suits and portable high-intensity light sources were deployed. The teams encountered significant resistance from the researcher, who was now fully under the influence of ABR-002. It took multiple personnel to subdue him using enhanced tactical measures. Restoration of full illumination was prioritized. Technicians worked under hazardous conditions to repair the damaged lighting system, successfully restoring it within 20 minutes. With full lighting reestablished, ABR-002 was forced back into a detectable state. Security teams used coordinated light sweeps to herd the entity back into its central containment area.

Post-Incident Analysis

The researcher was terminated on-site to prevent further neurological contamination and ensure the safety of containment personnel. Post-incident analysis confirmed that ABR-002 had deliberately extended tendrils towards high-traffic areas, indicating premeditated strategy and awareness of containment protocols. The damage assessment revealed critical vulnerabilities in the containment chamber design, particularly regarding the robustness of the LED lighting system against physical tampering.

Recommendations and Preventative Measures

  • Upgrade the containment chamber’s lighting system to include redundant, independently powered light sources to prevent total failure during similar incidents.
  • Implement additional layers of non-reflective, high-density polymer materials within the chamber to reduce ABR-002’s ability to manipulate its environment and personnel.
  • Enhance surveillance systems with multi-spectrum detection capabilities, including infrared and ultraviolet cameras, to ensure continuous tracking of ABR-002 despite its optical camouflage.
  • Conduct specialized training for all personnel interacting with high-risk Aberrations like ABR-002, focusing on rapid response to neurological and behavioral anomalies.
  • Establish a stricter clearance protocol for entry into high-threat containment chambers, ensuring only the most qualified and prepared individuals are granted access.

Long-Term Research and Containment Strategy

  • Prioritize ongoing research into ABR-002’s adaptive behaviors and cognitive functions to develop more effective containment strategies.
  • Regularly review and update containment protocols based on new findings from incident reports and research advancements.
  • Invest in advanced predictive analytics to anticipate potential breaches and preemptively adjust containment measures.
