ABERRATION DOCUMENT: ABR-019

Aberration Designation: ABR-019

Special Title: Deathly Snapshot

Threat Level: 04

Containment Division: Delta-3

ABR-019

Description

ABR-019, known as Deathly Snapshot, is a vintage Polaroid camera of unknown make and model. The camera exhibits no external markings or serial numbers, and its internal mechanisms do not correspond to any known manufacturing techniques. Despite its antiquated appearance, ABR-019 functions normally and produces instant photographs.

When a photograph is taken using ABR-019, the resulting Polaroid image will depict the scene as expected; however, any individuals present within the frame will appear to be deceased. The manner of death varies and can include violent injuries, natural causes, or unknown factors. The death depicted in the photograph is a predestined event that will occur to the subject at some point in the future.

Extensive testing has confirmed that the deaths predicted by ABR-019 are unavoidable. Even attempts to protect or isolate subjects from perceived threats have failed, with deaths occurring due to unforeseen circumstances or anomalies.

Containment Protocols

ABR-019 is to be stored in a high-security containment locker at ARI Site-19. Access to ABR-019 is restricted to Level 4 personnel and above, and any experimentation must be approved by the Site Director. All photographs taken by ABR-019 are to be stored in a separate secure file, accessible only with authorization from the Executive Council (C5).

Testing with ABR-019 is to be conducted in a controlled environment, with no fewer than three security personnel present. Subjects used for testing must be C0-class personnel. All testing sessions are to be recorded and documented. Under no circumstances is ABR-019 to be used outside of designated testing protocols.

Risk Assessment

ABR-019 demonstrates a consistent ability to reveal the moment of death at a given location, posing significant ethical and psychological risks. The potential for misuse or exploitation necessitates strict containment and access controls. The certainty of the events depicted, especially those predicted to occur within five years, presents profound implications for understanding and potentially manipulating fate and causality.

Behavioral Analysis

ABR-019 does not exhibit behavior but its effects on the environment it photographs are profound. The variability in the depicted deaths, combined with the certainty of occurrence within the five-year limit, suggests a deterministic aspect to ABR-019's function that defies conventional understanding of time and causality.

Appendices

Addendum ABR-019-1: Incident Report ABR-019-01

On ██/██/20██, during routine testing, ABR-019 was used to photograph a standard containment cell. The resulting image showed the cell empty, with no signs of death. However, during subsequent tests in different locations, the photographs consistently depicted deaths of individuals at those locations, confirming ABR-019's ability to reveal death scenes.

Addendum ABR-019-2: Experiment Log ABR-019-A

  • Location: Containment Cell A-19
  • Procedure: ABR-019 used to photograph the cell.
  • Result: Photograph showed no signs of death. The cell has no recorded incidents of death within the past or future five-year range.
  • Location: Site-19 Cafeteria
  • Procedure: ABR-019 used to photograph the cafeteria.
  • Result: Photograph depicted an individual suffering from extensive third-degree burns. A week later, a fire broke out in the cafeteria due to an electrical fault, resulting in injuries and confirming the scene depicted in the photograph.

Addendum ABR-019-3: Safety Protocol Improvement Initiative

ABR-019 has been utilized to enhance safety measures within high-risk containment cells. By taking images of these cells and observing any depicted deaths, ARI personnel have implemented additional safety protocols until photographs taken by ABR-019 no longer depict any deaths within the five-year range. This method has successfully reduced incidents and improved overall safety within these areas.

Conclusion: ABR-019 exhibits a consistent ability to reveal the moment of death at a given location within a five-year future range or any past event. The source of ABR-019's predictive capability remains unknown. Further study is required to understand the mechanism behind its anomalous properties and to explore potential uses or countermeasures.

Researcher's Note: The implications of ABR-019 are profound, suggesting a deterministic aspect of reality that defies conventional understanding. Ethical considerations must be paramount in all future experiments, given the inevitability of the outcomes depicted by ABR-019.

END OF DOCUMENT
