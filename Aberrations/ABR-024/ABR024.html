ABERRATION DOCUMENT: ABR-024

Aberration Designation: ABR-024

Threat Level: 07 (Severe Threat)

Containment Division: Delta-2 (High Risk)

Image of ABR-024

Special Containment Procedures

ABR-024 is to be monitored remotely via satellite and a dedicated observation vessel positioned at a safe distance of 10 nautical miles. Direct human interaction with ABR-024 is prohibited. Any ships or aircraft approaching within 5 nautical miles of ABR-024 are to be redirected by MTF Theta-4 ("Oil Slickers") under the guise of routine maritime patrols.

No crew is to be stationed on ABR-024. Any maintenance or investigations must be conducted by remote-controlled drones. Personnel assigned to ABR-024 must undergo psychological evaluation and training to avoid unconsciousness during operations.

Description

ABR-024, formerly designated as OR-273, is an offshore oil rig located in the Gulf of Mexico. ABR-024 exhibits anomalous behavior whereby it shifts its location by an average of 20 nautical miles whenever all individuals on board fall asleep simultaneously. These shifts occur instantaneously and without any detectable intermediate states.

ABR-024 came to the attention of the Aberration Research Institute on ██/██/20██ after a series of unusual reports from maritime tracking stations. The oil rig was reported missing and later reappeared several miles from its original location without any clear explanation. The crew was discovered to have fallen asleep due to an unknown cause, waking up in a different location.

On ██/██/20██, ABR-024 underwent a significant anomalous event. During this shift, the rig's drilling apparatus began extracting a substance identified as human blood (designated ABR-024-A) instead of crude oil. ABR-024-A has been confirmed to be of various blood types, though the source remains unidentified.

Analysis of ABR-024-A has revealed it to be chemically identical to human blood, with DNA analysis indicating multiple human donors. The volume of ABR-024-A extracted is far beyond what would be expected from a natural source, suggesting a potentially extradimensional or anomalous reservoir.

Addendum ABR-024-1: Incident Report ABR-024-14

On ██/██/20██, an unmanned drone was dispatched to ABR-024 to collect samples of ABR-024-A and to install additional surveillance equipment. During this operation, the drone experienced a temporary loss of signal. Upon re-establishing contact, the drone's footage revealed a drastic change in ABR-024's environment, now appearing in a dense fog with visibility reduced to a few meters.

Further analysis of the footage showed indistinct humanoid figures moving within the fog around ABR-024. These entities did not approach the drone and disappeared as the fog dissipated.

Addendum ABR-024-2: Research Log

Dr. ██████'s notes:

"We still have no explanation for the source of ABR-024-A or the mechanism behind ABR-024's shifts. The presence of humanoid figures in the fog suggests an intelligent or at least reactive component to this anomaly. Until we understand more, ABR-024 remains a high-priority subject for remote study. No personnel are to board the rig under any circumstances."

Addendum ABR-024-3: Exploration Proposal

Proposal to deploy a high-altitude drone equipped with advanced sensors to map the shifts and analyze the environmental changes during ABR-024's relocations. The aim is to understand the pattern, if any, behind its movements and the connection to ABR-024-A.

Approval pending Executive Council review.

Note: The discovery of ABR-024 and its ongoing analysis underscore the necessity of caution and thorough remote investigation to prevent further risks to human personnel and to uncover the true nature of this anomaly.

END OF DOCUMENT ABR-024
