ARI Aberration Threat Levels

Beneficial Levels

  • Threat Level: -05 - Exceptionally beneficial. Actively enhances the environment, health, or well-being on a significant scale.
  • Threat Level: -04 - Highly beneficial. Provides considerable positive effects with consistent, favorable outcomes.
  • Threat Level: -03 - Moderately beneficial. Offers noticeable improvements to localized areas or systems.
  • Threat Level: -02 - Slightly beneficial. Positive effects are present but limited in scope and impact.
  • Threat Level: -01 - Minor beneficial influence. Small-scale improvements with limited, yet positive, outcomes.

Neutral Levels

  • Threat Level: 00 - Neutral presence. No perceptible threat or benefit. Effects, if any, are inconsequential.

Negative Threat Levels

  • Threat Level: 01 - Negligible threat. Minimal impact with effects that are inconsequential.
  • Threat Level: 02 - Minimal threat with limited impact. Requires minimal observation and management.
  • Threat Level: 03 - Minor threat with localized effects. Easily managed with standard protocols.
  • Threat Level: 04 - Moderate threat. Potentially hazardous to personnel or infrastructure if mishandled.
  • Threat Level: 05 - Significant threat. Capable of causing serious harm or disruption. Requires rigorous containment measures.
  • Threat Level: 06 - High threat with severe consequences. Threatens life, property, or critical systems. Demands stringent containment and management.
  • Threat Level: 07 - Severe threat. Poses critical danger to large populations or essential systems. Requires immediate and comprehensive action.
  • Threat Level: 08 - Extreme threat. Capable of causing widespread destruction or significant loss of life. Requires top-priority containment and response.
  • Threat Level: 09 - Catastrophic threat. Presents an existential danger to regional stability or humanity at large. Containment may involve unprecedented measures.
  • Threat Level: 10 - Apocalyptic threat. Capable of global devastation or existential cosmic impact. Requires global coordination for containment and mitigation.
  • Threat Level: 11 - Existential Annihilation. Capable of annihilating not just the universe but the very fabric of existence.
