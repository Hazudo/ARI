About the Aberration Research Institute (ARI)

The Aberration Research Institute (ARI) is a specialized, independent organization committed to the investigation, research, understanding, and secure containment of anomalies, phenomena, and entities collectively referred to as "Aberrations."

Our Mission

Our mission encompasses:

  • Investigation: Conducting thorough examinations and inquiries into unexplained phenomena and Aberrations.
  • Research: Developing an in-depth understanding of Aberrations through rigorous scientific exploration and study.
  • Containment: Implementing secure measures to contain and control Aberrations, prioritizing the safety and well-being of humanity.

Ethical Guidelines

We operate under strict ethical guidelines to ensure the greater welfare and security of human civilization:

  • Humanity's Greater Good: We maintain a utilitarian approach, prioritizing actions and decisions that ensure the greater welfare and security of human civilization.
  • Sentient Aberrations: We adhere to humane treatment and respect for non-hostile sentient Aberrations, recognizing their unique attributes and potential contributions to our understanding.

Operational Scope

ARI operates on an interplanetary scale, with branches strategically located in different parts of various worlds, ensuring a comprehensive response to Aberrations. We work independently and discreetly, maintaining our autonomy without significant collaboration or dependence on external governments or entities.

Research and Containment Approach

  • Cautious Exploration: We prioritize containment and prevention of Aberration-related risks, focusing on ethical study and experimentation to understand and leverage the potential benefits of Aberrations.
  • Structured Response: We employ a tiered and systematic approach to Aberrations, tailored to the specific level of threat and complexity they pose.

Secrecy and Public Relations

Operating covertly, ARI utilizes disinformation, front organizations, and alternative narratives to maintain confidentiality and divert attention from its true purpose and activities.

Long-term Vision

ARI aspires to establish itself as the foremost expert on Aberrations worldwide, setting the standard for research, containment, and ethical engagement with these unparalleled phenomena.
