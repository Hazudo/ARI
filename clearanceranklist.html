Security Clearance Rank List

Clearance Level 6 (C6 - Censored and Unknown)

Description: Highest security level, known to select high-ranking officials. Strictly compartmentalized.

Clearance Level 5 (C5 - Executive Council)

Description: Executive Council members oversee critical decisions, classified information, and top-level initiatives.

Clearance Level 4 (C4 - Division Heads)

Description: Division Heads control departmental information, vital research data, and containment protocols. Key decision-makers.

Clearance Level 3 (C3 - Lead Researchers and Containment Specialists)

Description: Given to lead researchers, containment specialists, and agents. Access includes detailed operational information and sensitive data.

Clearance Level 2 (C2 - Support Personnel)

Description: Assigned to support staff (admin, analysts, security, tech support). Restricted to necessary information.

Clearance Level 1 (C1 - General Personnel)

Description: Covers general personnel with access to basic information. Involved in routine operations, no sensitive data.

Clearance Level 0 (C0 - Non-essential Personnel)

Description: Limited access to critical information. Includes non-essential personnel such as visitors and temporary staff. They are monitored and accompanied at all times to ensure security and safety.

Clearance Level -1 (E-Class - Expendable Personnel)

Main article: E-Class Personnel

Description: Includes individuals assigned to high-risk experiments and testing. Their participation is strictly regulated to minimize risks. Ethical protocols are reviewed by an Ethical Review Board to ensure well-being and consent.

Personnel Advancement and Training

Clearance levels can be adjusted based on performance, training, and qualifications. ARI provides a clear career progression path with regular reviews and targeted training to handle higher responsibilities. Personnel are encouraged to pursue additional qualifications and demonstrate competencies to achieve clearance upgrades, ensuring they are well-prepared for their roles.
