ARI Security Clearance Rank List

Clearance Level 6 (C6 - Censored and Unknown)

Description: The highest security level, known only to select high-ranking officials. Information is strictly compartmentalized, ensuring that only those with C6 clearance have access to the most sensitive and classified data. The specifics of C6 clearance are tightly controlled, with very few individuals aware of the full extent of this level's privileges and responsibilities.

Clearance Level 5 (C5 - Executive Council)

Description: Executive Council members oversee critical decisions, classified information, and top-level initiatives. This level involves strategic planning and implementation of high-impact projects. C5 clearance holders have comprehensive knowledge of ARI’s operations and are responsible for major policy decisions and the allocation of resources for aberration containment and research.

Clearance Level 4 (C4 - Division Heads)

Description: Division Heads control departmental information, vital research data, and containment protocols. They are key decision-makers within their respective departments, managing both personnel and operations to ensure effective research and containment strategies. C4 clearance holders play a crucial role in inter-departmental coordination and the execution of ARI's broader mission.

Clearance Level 3 (C3 - Lead Researchers and Containment Specialists)

Description: Assigned to lead researchers, containment specialists, and agents. Individuals with C3 clearance have access to detailed operational information and sensitive data necessary for their roles. They conduct in-depth research, develop containment protocols, and are actively involved in field operations, ensuring the safety and success of ARI’s missions.

Clearance Level 2 (C2 - Support Personnel)

Description: Assigned to support staff including administration, analysts, security, and tech support. Their access is restricted to the information necessary for their specific tasks. C2 personnel provide essential services that support the smooth operation of ARI, ensuring that critical functions are maintained and that higher-level personnel can focus on their specialized tasks.

Clearance Level 1 (C1 - General Personnel)

Description: Covers general personnel with access to basic information required for routine operations. C1 personnel do not handle sensitive data but perform essential tasks that keep ARI operational. They are involved in day-to-day activities, maintenance, and general support roles.

Clearance Level 0 (C0 - Non-essential Personnel)

Description: Limited access to critical information. Includes expendable personnel such as death row convicts, terminally ill individuals, and volunteers. This clearance level is regulated to minimize risks and ensure ethical standards are maintained, with protocols reviewed by an Ethical Review Board to safeguard the well-being and consent of these individuals.

Personnel Advancement and Training

Advancement: Clearance levels can be adjusted based on performance, training, and qualifications. ARI provides a clear career progression path, offering regular reviews and targeted training to prepare personnel for higher responsibilities. This system ensures that only the most capable and trusted individuals advance to higher clearance levels.

Training: Comprehensive training programs are designed to equip personnel with the necessary skills and knowledge to handle their duties effectively. This includes specialized courses in aberration research, containment protocols, emergency response, and ethical considerations. Continuous education and skill development are emphasized to maintain high standards of competence and readiness within ARI.
