Our Mission

The Aberration Research Institute (ARI) is dedicated to the investigation, research, understanding, and secure containment of anomalies, phenomena, and entities collectively referred to as "Aberrations." Our mission is to protect humanity from these unknown threats while advancing scientific knowledge.
