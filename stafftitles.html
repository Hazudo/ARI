Staff Titles

Executive Council (Clearance Level 5)

Director of Operations

Oversees all ARI operations and initiatives.

Chief Research Officer

Heads all research departments and projects.

Chief Containment Officer

Manages containment strategies and protocols.

Chief Security Officer

Responsible for the security of ARI facilities and personnel.

Chief Ethical Officer

Ensures all operations adhere to ethical standards.

Division Heads (Clearance Level 4)

Head of Research Division

Manages research activities across various departments.

Head of Containment Division

Oversees containment teams and ensures protocols are followed.

Head of Security Division

Leads security operations and emergency response teams.

Head of Administrative Services

Manages administrative support functions.

Head of Technological Development

Directs the development of new technologies for research and containment.

Lead Researchers and Containment Specialists (Clearance Level 3)

Lead Researcher

Supervises research projects and teams.

Senior Containment Specialist

Oversees containment operations for high-risk aberrations.

Field Agent Leader

Leads field operations for the capture and study of aberrations.

Senior Security Officer

Manages security personnel and operations.

Senior Analyst

Analyzes data and provides insights for research and containment efforts.

Support Personnel (Clearance Level 2)

Research Assistant

Assists researchers in their studies and experiments.

Containment Technician

Supports containment specialists in maintaining containment protocols.

Security Officer

Provides security and ensures the safety of ARI facilities and personnel.

Administrative Assistant

Offers administrative support to various departments.

Technical Support Specialist

Maintains and repairs technological equipment.

General Personnel (Clearance Level 1)

Junior Researcher

Conducts research under the supervision of lead researchers.

Containment Support Staff

Assists in basic containment operations and procedures.

Security Guard

Monitors and patrols ARI premises.

Administrative Clerk

Handles routine administrative tasks and documentation.

Lab Technician

Prepares and maintains laboratory equipment and supplies.

Non-essential Personnel (Clearance Level 0)

Expendable Personnel

Includes individuals with limited access, often used in high-risk situations.

Volunteer Subject

Participates in research studies under strict ethical guidelines.

Intern

Gains experience and assists with low-risk tasks and duties.
