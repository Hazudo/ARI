Staff Titles

Site Staff

Containment Specialist
Containment Specialists at ARI are critical for maintaining the integrity of our operations. Their primary responsibility is to secure, contain, and transport Aberrations from the field to ARI containment sites. These specialists are also tasked with designing, implementing, and maintaining containment protocols and units for all classified Aberrations within ARI facilities.

Researcher
Researchers are the intellectual backbone of ARI, composed of elite scientists from a variety of disciplines, including but not limited to, xenobiology, theoretical physics, and anomalous materials science. Their mission is to decode the mysteries of Aberrations and develop strategies to mitigate their threats.

Security Officer
Security Officers at ARI are highly trained professionals responsible for the physical and informational security of ARI sites. Recruited from military and law enforcement backgrounds, they are adept in combat and crisis management. These officers also ensure the integrity of sensitive information and are the first line of defense against internal and external threats.

Tactical Response Officer
Tactical Response Officers are elite combat units trained to handle high-risk situations involving dangerous Aberrations or hostile entities. They support Containment Specialists during the most perilous retrieval missions and defend ARI facilities against aggressive incursions.

Field Personnel

Field Agent
Field Agents are the eyes and ears of ARI, operating undercover within local law enforcement, emergency services, and regulatory bodies to detect and report signs of anomalous activity. Once identified, they coordinate with containment teams to secure the Aberrations.

Mobile Task Force Operative
Mobile Task Force Operatives are specialized units drawn from ARI's best field personnel. These operatives are assembled to address specific threats, from scientific researchers handling peculiar anomalies to heavily armed units dealing with dangerous entities. Each task force is tailored to a particular type of Aberration or threat.

Administration

Site Director
The Site Director is the highest-ranking official at ARI facilities, responsible for overseeing all operations, ensuring the safety of contained Aberrations, and managing all departmental directors. The Site Director reports directly to the Executive Council.

Executive Council Member
The Executive Council consists of the top echelon of ARI leadership. Council members have complete access to all information regarding Aberrations and oversee all ARI operations. Their identities are classified, and they operate under strict protocols to avoid direct contact with Aberrations.
